Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers regarding our purity certifications, delivery areas, and commercial services.

How do you verify the purity of your milk?
Every single batch of milk undergoes 12 comprehensive laboratory quality tests before packaging, checking for standard fat content, solid-not-fat (SNF) profiles, adulterants, urea, starch, and added water. Our premium Vedic A2 Gir Cow Milk is tested 100% organic and FSSAI certified, preserving fresh natural enzymes.
Do you offer free delivery for household orders?
Yes! We provide free doorstep shipping for all daily orders exceeding ₹500. For orders under ₹500, a minimal delivery charge of ₹40 is applied. Our delivery executives operate in early morning shifts (5:00 AM - 8:00 AM) to ensure you receive fresh dairy products daily.
What B2B wholesale services do you provide to restaurants?
We offer dedicated bulk supply accounts for cafes, bakeries, sweet shops, and luxury hotels. Our B2B clients benefit from custom volume pricing, stable morning slots, credit terms, and our specialized low-moisture malai paneer (zero crumbling under kitchen heat) and high-fat granular desi ghee. You can use our B2B Portal to forecast savings.
What is the shelf life of your paneer and milk?
Our fresh cow milk has a chilled shelf life of 72 hours. Our premium A2 Gir Cow Milk has a shelf life of 48 hours. Fresh malai paneer blocks remain fresh for up to 7 days if kept constantly refrigerated between 2°C to 4°C. No chemical preservatives are added.
